3 Reasons why people smoke

Addiction

Nicotine is the main addictive substance in cigarettes. It is a drug that affects many parts of your body, including your brain. Nicotine reaches your brain within 10 seconds of when it enters your body. It causes the brain to release adrenaline, and that creates a buzz of pleasure and energy.

Stress

Some people smoke when they feel stressed. They use smoking as a way to cope. Some smokers find it hard to give up cigarettes as a way to cope with stress.

Social

Some people smoke when hanging out with friends or to feel comfortable in social situations. Social smokers smoke occasionally and almost always in groups. For many people, doing a trigger activity they connect with smoking makes them want to smoke. However, there are health consequences if you smoke, even if it is only once in a while.
